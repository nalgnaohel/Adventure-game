Hello, this is an instruction for this game.

Controls:
- Use arrow keys(left, right) to move to the corresponding direction.
- Press up arrow key to jump
- P: pause game
- E: swift attack
- Q: create magic arrow
- S: ultimate - magic sphere. Although the damage is huge, please be aware that
the cooldown for magic sphere is 20s(maybe lower if you reach higher level).
You may increase your exp by killing ghosts(different type of ghosts will have
different exp value). 
Press Space to close this 'letter'. You may open it by pressing X.
